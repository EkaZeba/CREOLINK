Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and support

How quickly can I get fiber optic internet installed?

Standard installations are typically completed within 3-5 business days after order confirmation. For business installations, the timeline may vary based on infrastructure requirements. We offer expedited installation for an additional fee.

What's the difference between fiber optic and traditional broadband?

Fiber optic internet uses light signals through glass fibers, providing much faster speeds (up to 1 Gbps), lower latency, and greater reliability compared to traditional copper-based broadband. It's also less susceptible to weather interference and offers symmetrical upload/download speeds.

Do you offer service bundles with TV and internet?

Yes! We offer several bundle options combining our NumeroLink TV service with HomeLink internet at discounted rates. Our most popular bundle includes 150 Mbps internet with 150+ TV channels. Contact our sales team for custom bundle options.

What kind of technical support do you provide?

We provide 24/7 technical support through multiple channels: phone, email, WhatsApp, and in-person visits for critical issues. Our support includes troubleshooting, equipment configuration, network optimization, and emergency repairs. Business customers receive dedicated account managers and priority support.

Can I upgrade my plan later if I need more speed?

Absolutely! You can upgrade your plan at any time without additional installation fees. Most upgrades are activated within 24 hours. We also offer flexible scaling options for business customers to accommodate growing bandwidth needs.
